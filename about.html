Joseph -

Hello! I'm Joseph, one of the two photographers at LJ.Photography.


At 13 years old, I have had 1 year of experience in photography, this includes aviation photography and general photography. I was born and raised in Christchurch, New Zealand.


The thing that sparked my interest in photography was when I went plane spotting for the first time. I liked plane spotting more than photography at the time, but then I bought a camera and from there I developed a hobby for photography. Now I do both general and aviation photography.


Throughout my time as a photographer, I have had 2 cameras, my first camera was a Nikon D3100. Which wasn't really good but good at the start. Then half a year later I bought a new Canon 200D Mark II. If you'd like to see some of my photos just check out the "photos" page and you'll see some of Lucas' and I's work.


Ever since I really got into photography, I've always wanted to start a business like this. The thing that made starting this business even better was the fact I was doing it with one of my good friends.
